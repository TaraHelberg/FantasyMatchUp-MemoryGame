How To Play

Welcome

  1. Click on the cards to flip them over
  2. Match 2 Pairs,only two cards can be clicked at a time
  3. Match pairs will stay up , unmatched will flip over
  4. You will be timed, try to beat your time :D
  5. The number of times you flip cards is tracked see if you can beat it :D
